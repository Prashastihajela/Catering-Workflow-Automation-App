# Overview

The Catering App is a thoughtfully designed solution aimed at streamlining the entire catering process by providing an intuitive, all-in-one platform for service providers and clients. It enables seamless event coordination, efficient request handling, and real-time menu exploration, eliminating the fragmentation and inefficiencies that often plague traditional catering management. In the fast-paced hospitality industry, small catering businesses managing multiple concurrent events with limited staff struggle with operational inefficiencies that hinder growth and service quality.

- Key Business Challenges
    - **Scattered Request Management:** Difficulty Managing Requests arriving through multiple channels such as phone calls, emails, social media messages, and website forms, leading to redundant effort, potential oversights, and increased risk of double-bookings or missed opportunities.
    - **Wasted Time on Quote Inquiries** from clients who may only need a price estimate for internal approval, consuming valuable staff hours that could be better spent on confirmed event planning and execution. This challenge is particularly acute during peak seasons when quick response times are crucial.
    - **Administrative Overload** stemming from repeated inquiries about the same events, creating extra work and stress for staff, while also increasing the likelihood of miscommunication and errors in event details and requirements.
    
    By addressing these fundamental operational challenges, the Catering App aims to eliminate the chaos in traditional event and catering management, allowing businesses to focus on what they do best: creating memorable experiences for their clients.
    

### **Target Users**

Catering Companies & Professionals, Restaurant Owners, Event Planners, Freelance Caterers & Home Chefs: This app is designed to offer a ***one-stop shopping experience***—from introducing your services and exploring menus to requesting and managing events. It provides a versatile solution for anyone offering catering services for private or corporate events, bringing all essential functions together in one place.

- **Planning**: During this phase, the project’s objectives, scope, and constraints were defined.
    
    ### **Key Considerations:**
    
    ✅ **Cost Efficiency** – The app is built using Microsoft Power Apps & SharePoint within an existing Microsoft 365 subscription, eliminating additional investment.
    
    ✅ **Scope** – Convert existing app into a Responsive app. Power BI dashboards are excluded in this phase but planned for future enhancements.
    
    ✅ **Stakeholders** – As a solo developer, I handled requirement gathering, design, and development, while key personnel (Catering Manager, Sales Supervisor, Director) provided insights to ensure business alignment.
    
- **Design**: This phase focused on analyzing business needs, selecting the right technology, and designing the app's structure.
    
    **Technology Decisions:**
    
    - **Low-Code Approach:** Power Apps was chosen due to:
        - Ease of maintenance & modification
        - Seamless Microsoft 365 integration
        - Faster development & deployment
    
    **Development Tools:**
    
    - **Power Apps (Canvas App)** – UI & front-end design
    - **SharePoint Lists** – Backend for storing catering requests & menu items
    - **Power Automate (Future Use)** – Automating approvals & notifications
    
- **Implementation:** The development phase focused on building a user-friendly catering request app with intuitive navigation & efficient workflows.
    
    ### **Core Functionalities:**
    
    - **Catering Request Submission Form** – Allows users to book catering services.
    - **Menu Display with Pricing & Selection** – Users can browse and add items.
    - **Quote Generation & Summary** – Auto-calculates total cost and displays final order details.
    - **Shopping Cart Model** – One stop shopping experience.

### **Key Features**

- Real-Time Menu Updates
- One-Stop Shopping Experience
- Single Submission, No Redundancy
- Automated Quote Summary Generator
- SharePoint Integration for managing data.
- Guests can submit event request details like event name, date, time, and menu and can review their submission.
- Intended Audience: Students, Staff, Faculty.

## **Impact & Business Value**

- 90% Reduction in manual request handling through centralized request management.
- Increased Operational Efficiency, allowing small teams to handle more orders with less administrative burden.
- Faster Response Times, improving customer experience and increasing client retention.
- Minimized Errors, ensuring accurate, streamlined communication between catering teams and customers.
